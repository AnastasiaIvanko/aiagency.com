Stages of site development

Start
Just write me
Text me on Telegram, WhatsApp, Viber or email me.
You compose and send me the terms of reference. Or just describe what type of site and why you need, preferred style, colors, examples of sites that you like, as well as the logo, texts, graphics, photos, etc
If something is missing - ok, I'll do it and pick it up myself.
Prepayment
I am making a website
For website creation, we require a prepayment of either 30% of the total cost for a landing page or 50% for the main page of multipage websites.
1st screen ready
When I complete the main page - the most important part of any site, I give you the link for review and agreement. Either in video or text format, I explain the design and location of the elements.
All decisions made during the development of the site will be justified, each of them will contribute to the achievement of the goal (sales, service orders, subscriptions, etc.)
Next step
PC version of the site is ready
I will completely make the PC version of the site (this is the version for computers and laptops). I'm also sending you a ready version, so you can approve or make changes.
You can pay another 25% of the cost of the project.
At the finish line
Adaptive ready
Tablet and mobile versions are ready (at the same time, you need to understand that the site will be the same, it just adapts to different screens and devices).
And you pay another 25% of the project cost.
Finish
And here is your website
Next, I will proceed to the final stage of development (I will insert a map with your label, set up sending applications to your mail, optimize the code, compress pictures, etc.) after which I will make an archive with the site.
Additional services
If necessary, I will register a domain (for you) and hosting for a small fee, upload the site, set up.
It's free for you!
You will also have an admin panel with which you can change any text on the site, pictures, insert metric codes, etc.
Support
Always in touch and ready to help
Ordering my website, you can be sure that I will always help you if you need it. Update the description, add a new range, upload new pictures, change your phone number - just let me know.
This, of course, is not free, but you will be sure that if necessary to change something, nothing will break and everything will work well.
So write - I'm sure we'll work together!
